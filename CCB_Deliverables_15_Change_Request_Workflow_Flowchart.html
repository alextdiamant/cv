1
Initial Submission — Sections 1 & 2
Change Owner completes Change Request Details & Change Justification
👤 Change Owner 📝 Sections 1–2
📝 Section 1: Change Request Details CR ID, Title, Category, Priority, Environment
Section 1 — Change Request Details
Change Owner enters: CR ID (auto-generated), Title, Description, Category (Standard/Normal/Major/Emergency), Priority, Target Environment(s), Requested Implementation Date.
📋 Section 2: Change Justification Business Case, Impact, Stakeholders
Section 2 — Change Justification
Change Owner documents: Business justification, Expected benefits, Affected stakeholders, Urgency drivers, Alignment with strategic goals.
⚙️ Auto-Generate CR ID System
Automated CR ID
System generates unique CR ID in format: CR-YYYY-NNNN (e.g., CR-2026-0042). Timestamp and requestor metadata recorded.
📧 Submission Confirmation Email To: Change Owner, CCB Facilitator
📧 Email: Submission Confirmation
To: Change Owner, CCB Facilitator
Trigger: Sections 1–2 submitted
Content: CR ID, summary, next steps, link to form
⬇️
🔒 Dependency Gate: Sections 1–2 must be fully complete before routing to Technical Lead and Approvals
⬇️
2
Technical Details — Sections 3, 4 & 5
Technical Lead completes Risk Assessment, Technical Plan & Rollback
🔧 Technical Lead 📝 Sections 3–5
⚠️ Section 3: Risk Assessment Summary Business Impact, Rollback Complexity, Risk Level
Section 3 — Risk Assessment
Technical Lead assesses: Business Impact (1–5), Rollback Complexity (1–5), auto-calculated Overall Risk Level, Mitigation Strategies (required if Critical).
🔧 Section 4: Technical Implementation Technical Approach, Dependencies, Timeline
Section 4 — Technical Implementation
Technical Lead documents: Implementation approach, Technical dependencies, Step-by-step plan, Required resources, Estimated downtime.
🔄 Section 5: Rollback & Recovery Plan Rollback Procedure, Backup Details
Section 5 — Rollback & Recovery
Technical Lead provides: Rollback procedure, Backup and recovery details, Recovery time estimates, Communication plan for rollback.
📧 Technical Assignment Email To: Technical Lead
📧 Email: Technical Assignment
To: Technical Lead
Trigger: Sections 1–2 approved for routing
Content: CR details, sections to complete, deadline
⬇️
🔒 Dependency Gate: All sections (1–5) must be complete before routing to Approval Groups
⬇️
3
Approval Workflow — Section 9 (Automated)
Parallel approval requests sent to Business PO, Solution Engineering, QA Lead, UAT Lead
✅ Approval Groups ⚙️ Automated
📧 Approval Request Emails To: Business PO, SE, QA, UAT
📧 Email: Approval Requests
To: Business Product Owner, Solution Engineering Lead, QA Lead, UAT Lead
Trigger: All sections complete
Content: CR summary, link to review, approve/reject buttons
👤 Business PO Review Approve / Reject / Defer
Business PO Review
Business Product Owner reviews change justification, business impact, and alignment with business priorities. Can Approve, Reject, or Defer.
⚙️ Solution Engineering Review Approve / Reject / Defer
Solution Engineering Review
SE Lead reviews technical approach, architecture impact, and implementation feasibility. Can Approve, Reject, or Defer.
🧪 QA Lead Review Approve / Reject / Defer
QA Lead Review
QA Lead reviews test coverage, regression risk, and quality assurance plan. Can Approve, Reject, or Defer.
🧑‍💻 UAT Lead Review Approve / Reject / Defer
UAT Lead Review
UAT Lead reviews user acceptance criteria, testing plan, and end-user impact. Can Approve, Reject, or Defer.

4
CCB Meeting & Decision
CCB Facilitator schedules meeting, board reviews and decides
🏛️ CCB Facilitator ⚙️ Automated Agenda
📧 CCB Meeting Agenda Email T-48 hours • To: All CCB Members
📧 Email: CCB Agenda
To: All CCB Members
Trigger: 48 hours before scheduled CCB meeting
Content: Meeting agenda, CRs to review, supporting docs, dial-in details
🏛️ CCB Meeting Review all change requests
CCB Meeting
Change Control Board convenes to review change requests. Members discuss risk, impact, resource requirements. Each CR receives a formal decision.
◆ CCB Decision Approved / Rejected / Deferred
CCB Decision Point
Board votes on each CR:
✅ Approved — proceed to implementation
❌ Rejected — returned with rationale
⏸️ Deferred — postponed with conditions
📧 CCB Decision Notification To: Change Owner, Stakeholders
📧 Email: CCB Decision
To: Change Owner, All Stakeholders
Trigger: Decision recorded
Content: Decision (Approved/Rejected/Deferred), conditions, next steps
✅ Approved
Proceed to Implementation Planning (Stage 5). Change Owner and Technical Lead coordinate scheduling.
❌ Rejected
Returned to Change Owner with rationale. May resubmit after addressing concerns. Status → Closed-Rejected.
⏸️ Deferred
Postponed with conditions. Reviewed again at next CCB meeting. Change Owner notified of deferral reasons.
⬇️
🔒 Dependency Gate: CCB Approval required before proceeding to Implementation
⬇️
5
Implementation — Sections 6 & 7
Change Owner + Technical Lead coordinate implementation, QA testing & UAT sign-off
👤 Change Owner 🔧 Technical Lead 📝 Sections 6–7
📧 T-7 Day Implementation Notice To: All Stakeholders
📧 Email: T-7 Day Notice
To: All Stakeholders
Trigger: 7 days before implementation
Content: Upcoming change details, timeline, expected impact
📧 T-3 Day Reminder To: Technical Team, Change Owner
📧 Email: T-3 Day Reminder
To: Technical Team, Change Owner
Trigger: 3 days before implementation
Content: Final preparations checklist, go/no-go confirmation
📧 T-1 Day Final Notice To: All Stakeholders
📧 Email: T-1 Day Final Notice
To: All Stakeholders
Trigger: 1 day before implementation
Content: Final notice, maintenance window, rollback readiness
🚀 Section 6: Implementation Execution Implementation details, actual dates
Section 6 — Implementation
Change Owner + Technical Lead record: Actual implementation date/time, Steps executed, Issues encountered, Stakeholders notified.
🧪 Section 7: QA & Testing Sign-Off Product Owner, QA, SE status
Section 7 — QA Approval
Testing sign-offs recorded: Product Owner Testing Status, QA Testing Status, Solution Engineering Testing Status. All must pass before closure.
⬇️
🔒 Dependency Gate: Implementation must be complete and verified before PIR
⬇️
6
Post-Implementation Review — Section 8
Change Owner completes PIR (mandatory for Normal/Major changes)
👤 Change Owner 🔧 Technical Lead 📝 Section 8
📧 PIR Reminder Email To: Change Owner
📧 Email: PIR Reminder
To: Change Owner
Trigger: Post-implementation (configurable: 3–7 days after)
Content: Reminder to complete PIR section, link to form
📊 Section 8: PIR Assessment Outcome, Lessons Learned, Follow-up
Section 8 — PIR Plan
Change Owner + Technical Lead complete: Implementation outcome assessment, Lessons learned, Follow-up actions required, Recommendations for future changes. Mandatory for Normal/Major changes.
⚙️ PIR Tracking System monitors completion
Automated PIR Tracking
System tracks PIR completion status. Sends follow-up reminders if not completed within SLA. Reports to CCB Facilitator for overdue PIRs.
⬇️
🔒 Dependency Gate: PIR must be complete before formal closure
⬇️
7
Closure & Notifications
Change Request formally closed; all stakeholders notified
⚙️ Automated ✅ Complete
⚙️ Status → Closed System updates register
Automated Closure
System updates CR status to "Closed-Successful" or "Closed-Failed". Updates Change Request Register. Records final metrics (total time, approval time, implementation time).
📧 Closure Notification Email To: All Stakeholders
📧 Email: Closure Notification
To: Change Owner, Technical Lead, Approvers, CCB Facilitator, All Stakeholders
Trigger: PIR complete & CR closed
Content: Final status, outcome summary, PIR findings, metrics
🏁 Change Request Complete Archived in Register
Process Complete
Change Request is fully closed. All documentation archived. Metrics captured for CCB Dashboard reporting. Lessons learned catalogued for future reference.
📈 Dashboard Metrics Updated Automated reporting
Metrics Update
CCB Metrics Dashboard automatically updated with: Completion metrics, Time-to-approval, Success/failure rates, Risk distribution changes.

❓ Flowchart Guide

How to Use This Flowchart

  • Stages 1–7 show the complete lifecycle of a Change Request from submission to closure.
  • 🔒 Dependency Gates between stages show what must be completed before proceeding.
  • Hover over any colored card to see a quick tooltip with details.
  • Click any card to see a full email content example (for email cards) or detailed information.

Color Legend

  • ■ Blue — Form completion activities
  • ■ Orange — Email notifications
  • ■ Purple — Approval activities
  • ■ Red — Decision points
  • ■ Grey — System/automated actions
  • ■ Green — Success/completion

Role Assignments

  • 👤 Change Owner — Sections 1, 2, 6, 7, 8
  • 🔧 Technical Lead — Sections 3, 4, 5 (+ assists 6, 7, 8)
  • ✅ Approval Groups — Section 9 (Business PO, SE, QA, UAT)
  • 🏛️ CCB Facilitator — Oversees process, chairs CCB meetings
  • ⚙️ System — Automated emails, reminders, tracking
